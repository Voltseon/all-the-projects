<big>'''Piloswine'''</big>}}'''Piloswine''' is a Ice/Ground-type Pokémon introduced in [[Pokémon and the Last Wish: Part II]].

It evolves from [[Swinub]] starting at level 33 and evolves into [[Mamoswine]] when leveled up while knowing Ancient Power.

==Game data==

===Pokédex entry===
<blockquote>A Piloswine is covered by a thick coat of long hair for enduring freezing cold. It uses its tusks to dig up food that has been buried under ice.</blockquote>
