<big>'''Haunter'''</big>}}'''Haunter''' is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in [[Pokémon and the Last Wish: Part II]].

It evolves from [[Gastly]] starting at level 25 and evolves into [[Gengar]] starting at level 35.

==Game data==

===Pokédex entry===
<blockquote>If a Haunter beckons you while it is floating in darkness, don't approach it. This Pokémon will try to lick you with its tongue and steal your life away.</blockquote>
